Website Plan: Discover Pampanga

Site Name

Name: Discover Pampanga: The Culinary Capital of the Philippines

This name reflects Pampanga's rich cultural heritage, famous landmarks, and its historic status as the culinary epicenter of the country. It invites tourists and locals to explore its vibrant festivals, historic churches, and world-class food scene.

Optional Domain: discoverpampanga.ph

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an interactive digital guide highlighting Pampanga’s top destinations, heritage sites, eco-adventures, and iconic Kapampangan dishes. It aims to boost local tourism, promote cultural preservation, and help travelers plan their ultimate trip.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist destinations and heritage sites I can visit in Pampanga?
  • Where can I taste authentic Kapampangan food and learn about local culinary traditions?

Color Scheme

These colors reflect Pampanga's warm hospitality, rich heritage, and vibrant food culture:

  • Warm Crimson / Flame (#D9381E) & Sunset Gold (#E67E22): Used for call-to-action buttons, active navigation, badges, and primary accents.
  • Soft Ivory (#FDFBF7): Used for clean section backgrounds and overall page canvas.
  • Deep Heritage Charcoal (#1F2421): Used for main headers, footers, and crisp readable typography.

Typography

  • Segoe UI / Arial / Sans-Serif: Used across headers, content boxes, and navigation menus to maintain a modern, clean, and scannable visual hierarchy.
